# Mentored Christian on Terraform for the Keycloak project

**Headline:** Coached a colleague to stand up his own IaC-managed Databricks workspace for the OIDC/Keycloak project.

## Context

The Keycloak project members needed infrastructure stood up but had no Terraform experience and no isolated place to experiment.

## Action

Interviewed them on their needs and determined a separate Databricks workspace on analytics-sandbox was the right call for a confined blast radius. Set up a pairing session, provisioned the right access, demonstrated the IaC workflow, then handed the keyboard over so they drove the actual stand-up.

## Result

They successfully provisioned a brand-new workspace themselves and can now iterate without coordinating with the existing team.
