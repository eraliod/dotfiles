# Cut Redshift spend by right-sizing unused clusters

**Headline:** Reduced monthly Redshift spend 32% by right-sizing and pausing idle clusters.

## Context

Redshift costs were climbing with no owner watching utilization, and several clusters sat near-idle outside business hours.

## Action

Audited cluster utilization, identified consistently idle capacity, and right-sized two clusters while adding a pause schedule for off-hours.

## Result

Monthly Redshift spend dropped by $4,200 (32%) with no impact on query SLAs.
